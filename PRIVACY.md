# Privacy Policy for LLM Friendly

Last Updated: June 2026

## Data Collection

* The extension does not collect personal information.
* The extension does not transmit files, document contents, or browsing data to servers operated by the developer.

## Local Storage

* User settings, enabled websites, and conversion history may be stored locally using the browser's storage APIs.
* This data remains on the user's device and is not transmitted by the extension.

## File Processing

* All document conversion and OCR processing occur locally within the browser.
* Captured files and extracted content are never uploaded to external servers by the extension.

## AI Website Interactions

* LLM Friendly is designed to help users prepare and format content for use with AI-powered websites and applications.
* When a user chooses to paste, submit, or upload content to an AI service or other website, that service may process, store, or transmit the content according to its own privacy policy and terms of service.
* Such transmissions are initiated by the user's interaction with the destination website and are not performed independently by the extension.

## Third Parties

* The extension does not sell, share, or transfer user data to third parties.
* The extension does not use external analytics, tracking, or advertising services.
